Planting the first leaves

Day 1 — early afternoon

Today I created the first folder and called it ecoquiet. It feels like the entrance to a tiny studio — quiet, warm, and full of potential.

The file explorer is the first leaf. A simple left column, a calm right column, and a few tiny pages pretending to be a workspace.

ecoQuiet is my attempt to bring a little studio warmth into the browser.
